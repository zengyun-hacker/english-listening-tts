# 示例一：情景对话（Dialogue）
# 适用于：高中/四六级 Part 1 短对话
@title: Unit 5 - At the Restaurant
@type: dialogue

Number 1.
[Man]: Good evening. Do you have a reservation?
[Woman]: Yes, I booked a table for two under the name Johnson.
[Man]: Ah, yes, right this way. Would you prefer a window seat?
[Woman]: That would be lovely, thank you.

Number 2.
[Man]: Are you ready to order, or do you need a few more minutes?
[Woman]: I think I'll have the grilled salmon, please.
[Man]: Excellent choice. And would you like anything to drink?
[Woman]: Just sparkling water, please.
